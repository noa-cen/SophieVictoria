Cross-Country Skiing

This project captures the unique atmosphere of cross-country skiing as a winter tourism activity. Through my images, I aimed to evoke the serenity and emotion of gliding through snowy landscapes, presenting it as an experience worth exploring. Designed as promotional content, it reflects my ability to highlight moments that invite viewers to immerse themselves in the adventure.
